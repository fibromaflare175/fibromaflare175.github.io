📖 What is EndNote?

EndNote helps you organize your research. You store your sources in one library. This software creates citations for your papers in seconds. It saves time when you write reports or articles. You avoid manual typing errors. Most users choose it for its simple interface and powerful tools.

🔍 Key Features

The software offers many tools for your daily tasks:

  • Library Management: Store all your journal articles and books.
  • Auto Citation: Insert quotes into your document directly from the software.
  • Style Formatting: Choose standard formats like APA, MLA, or Chicago with one click.
  • Full Text Search: Find specific notes inside your saved documents.

🛠️ Using the Software

Start by creating a new library file. Name the file and save it in a location you remember. Click the import button to add your local files into the system. The software reads your files and extracts the author, year, and title data. You see this data in the main view screen. To use these in your text editor, select the entry and press the copy button. Paste the formatted citation into your main report.
